Hi there! I'm Jacopo Tomasone, a web developer from Rome!

In the last 10+ years, I've spent most of my time staring at variously sized screens trying to make internet a better place, or at least less sucky .

I love handcrafting websites, and I mean it literally. I've always been fascinated by how various codes intertwine between themselves and suddenly here you go: a website! It's like magic, except I'm actually able to do it, without throwing countless decks of cards and a couple of doves out of the window.

When I'm not hands deep in dirt developing, I'm basically a whatever junkie: may it be videogames, tv series, comic books, proper books, music - you know, whatever - I'm there recklessly consuming them.
